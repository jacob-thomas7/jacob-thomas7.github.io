About

My name is Jacob Thomas, and I am a Computer Science Student attending Boise State University.

Computer Science

I've always wanted to be a programmer, even as early as elementary school. I was inspired by the engineering behind video games, particularily retro games, and wanted to create my own games. As I grew and learned to program, I found a love for the craft itself; the problem solving, the community, and the satisfaction of a finished program were all things I wanted to be involved in.

Music

Alongside my career focus in computer science, I am also an avid musician! My primary musical experience is in drumline, mallet percussion, and piano. I am also practicing the organ and other percussion instruments. I currently play snare drum for the Boise State Blue Thunder Marching Band. I also enjoy composing music; I have published several pieces. Finally, I teach younger percussionsts to help them develop their own skills.
